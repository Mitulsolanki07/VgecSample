Admissions

The institute runs 9 Under Graduate (UG) engineering programs and 2 Post Graduate (PG) engineering programs. Out of 9 UG programs, four programs are accredited by the NBA

Under Graduate Programs

Admission process to all UG programs is carried out by the state level Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC). For more details, visit ACPC website www.jacpcldce.ac.in/be.asp
